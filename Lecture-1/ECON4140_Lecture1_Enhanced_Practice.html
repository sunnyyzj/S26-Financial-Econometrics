2. Probability vs Statistics

ProbabilityStatistics
已知总体或模型，推断样本结果已知样本数据，推断总体特征
Parameter: \( \mu, \sigma^2, \sigma \)Estimator: \( \bar{x}, s^2, s \)
考试提醒：题目问 “all population / entire group” 时，通常使用 population parameter；题目问 “sample data” 时，使用 sample statistic。

Practice 1

某大学全部校友平均年龄为 50 岁。这个数值应该表示 \( \bar{x} \) 还是 \( \mu \)？

这是 \( \mu \)，因为它描述的是全部校友，即 population mean。

3. Measures of Central Tendency

3.1 Mean

Mean 表示数据的平均水平，在金融中可用于表示资产长期平均收益率。

Population Mean: \( \mu = \frac{\sum x_i}{N} \)
Sample Mean: \( \bar{x} = \frac{\sum x_i}{n} \)

Practice 2

数据为 2, 4, 6, 8, 10。求 sample mean。

\( \bar{x} = \frac{2+4+6+8+10}{5}=6 \)

3.2 Median

Median 是排序后位于中间位置的数据。Median 对 outliers 更稳健。

Practice 3

数据为 1, 2, 3, 4, 100。求 mean 和 median，并说明哪个更适合描述数据中心。

Mean = 22，Median = 3。由于 100 是极端值，median 更适合描述中心位置。

3.3 Trimmed Mean

Trimmed mean 会去掉两端一定比例的极端值，再计算平均数，是 mean 和 median 之间的折中。

R Example:
x <- c(80,80,80,80,80,81,82,78,79,70,73,74,64,45)
mean(x, trim = 0.15)

Central Tendency — Practice Bank

Practice 3A — Mean vs. Median and Skewness

A sample of families has mean family size 3.2, median 2, and mode 2. What is the likely shape of the distribution?

The distribution is likely right-skewed. Since mean > median, a small number of large families pulls the mean upward.

Practice 3B — Trimmed Mean

A dataset has \(n=20\). For a 15% trimmed mean, how many observations should be removed from each tail?

\(0.15 \times 20 = 3\). Remove the lowest 3 and highest 3 observations, then average the remaining 14 observations.

Practice 3C — Robustness

A wage dataset has one extremely high income. Which measure is more robust: mean or median? Why?

The median is more robust because it depends on the middle position after sorting, while the mean uses every value and is pulled by outliers.

Practice 4

股票 A 和 B 的平均收益率均为 10%。A 的标准差为 2%，B 的标准差为 8%。哪个风险更高？

B 风险更高，因为 B 的标准差更大，说明收益波动更强。

4.2 Coefficient of Variation

\( CV=\frac{s}{\bar{x}} \)

CV 衡量单位收益对应的风险，适合比较平均收益不同的资产。

CV = 0.20

Spread, Quartiles & CV — Practice Bank

Practice 4A — Range and IQR

Five-number summary: Min = 20, Q1 = 35, Median = 42, Q3 = 50, Max = 90. Compute range and IQR. Which one is more robust?

Range \(=90-20=70\). IQR \(=50-35=15\). The IQR is more robust because it focuses on the middle 50% and ignores extreme values.

Practice 4B — Degrees of Freedom

A sample has \(n=8\). What is the degree of freedom used in the sample variance formula? Why?

\(df=n-1=7\). Once \(\bar{x}\) is estimated, the deviations from the mean must sum to zero, so only 7 deviations are free to vary.

Practice 4C — Coefficient of Variation

Asset A has mean return 8% and standard deviation 2%. Asset B has mean return 15% and standard deviation 4%. Which asset has higher relative risk?

\(CV_A=2/8=0.25\). \(CV_B=4/15\approx0.267\). Asset B has slightly higher relative risk per unit of return.

Practice 4D — Choosing Summary Statistics

If a dataset is strongly right-skewed, should we summarize it using mean and standard deviation, or median and IQR?

Use median and IQR. For skewed data, mean and standard deviation are sensitive to extreme values.

5. Normal Distribution & Empirical Rule

RangeApproximate Probability
\( \mu \pm 1\sigma \)68%
\( \mu \pm 2\sigma \)95%
\( \mu \pm 3\sigma \)99.7%

Practice 5

已知 mean return = 5%，standard deviation = 2%。若 returns 近似服从 normal distribution，收益落在 1% 到 9% 之间的概率约为多少？

1% = \(5\%-2\times2\%\)，9% = \(5\%+2\times2\%\)，所以区间为 \( \mu \pm 2\sigma \)，概率约为 95%。

5.1 Standardization and Z-Scores

\( z=\frac{x-\bar{x}}{s} \)

A z-score tells how many standard deviations an observation is above or below the mean. Standardization changes the scale, but not the shape of the distribution.

z = 1.40

Normal Distribution, Z-Score & Histogram — Practice Bank

Practice 5A — Empirical Rule

Starting salaries have mean $50,000 and standard deviation $10,000. Assume a bell-shaped distribution. Approximately what percent of salaries are between $30,000 and $70,000?

$30,000 and $70,000 are \(\mu \pm 2\sigma\). By the empirical rule, about 95% of salaries fall in this range.

Practice 5B — Tail Probability by Empirical Rule

Using the same salary distribution, approximately what percent of salaries are above $70,000?

Above $70,000 is above \(\mu+2\sigma\). About 5% lies outside \(\mu\pm2\sigma\), split equally between two tails, so the answer is about 2.5%.

Practice 5C — Z-Score Interpretation

A student scores 84 on a test with mean 72 and standard deviation 6. Compute and interpret the z-score.

\(z=(84-72)/6=2\). The score is 2 standard deviations above the mean.

Practice 5D — Histogram Shape

A histogram has two clear peaks. What does this suggest about the data?

It suggests a bimodal distribution, often because the dataset combines two different subgroups.

6. Financial Returns

6.1 Net Return

\( R_t=\frac{P_t-P_{t-1}}{P_{t-1}} \)

6.2 Gross Return

\( 1+R_t=\frac{P_t}{P_{t-1}} \)

6.3 Log Return

\( r_t=\ln\left(\frac{P_t}{P_{t-1}}\right) \)
为什么金融计量喜欢 log return？Log returns 具有可加性，更适合多期收益和时间序列建模。

Financial Returns — Practice Bank

Practice 6A — Net and Gross Return

A stock price increases from 50 to 55. Compute the net return and gross return.

Net return \(R=(55-50)/50=0.10=10\%\). Gross return \(1+R=55/50=1.10\).

Practice 6B — Log Return

A stock price increases from 100 to 120. Compute the log return.

\(r=\ln(120/100)=\ln(1.2)\approx0.1823=18.23\%\).

Practice 6C — Multi-period Log Return

Three daily log returns are 0.01, -0.02, and 0.03. What is the three-day log return?

Multi-period log returns are additive: \(0.01-0.02+0.03=0.02\), or approximately 2%.

7. Random Walk & EMH

7.1 Random Walk

\( P_t=P_{t-1}+u_t \)

Random walk 表示价格变化主要来自随机冲击，过去价格不能稳定预测未来价格。

7.2 Efficient Market Hypothesis

如果市场有效，所有公开信息已经反映在价格中，因此投资者无法长期稳定获得 abnormal return。

Practice 7

如果 log-prices follow a random walk，过去价格是否能稳定预测未来价格？

不能。Random walk 暗示价格变化不可预测，未来价格主要由新的随机信息决定。

Integrated Review Questions

Review Q1 — Parameter or Statistic?

A researcher reports the average return of all stocks in an index. Is this closer to \( \mu \) or \( \bar{x} \)?

It is closer to \( \mu \), because it describes the entire population/index being considered.

Review Q2 — Outlier Effect

If the maximum salary in a dataset increases from $120,000 to $200,000, what happens to mean, median, range, IQR, and standard deviation?

Mean increases; median stays about the same; range increases; IQR stays about the same; standard deviation increases.

Review Q3 — Best Summary Choice

For a roughly symmetric return distribution, which pair is usually appropriate: mean/s.d. or median/IQR?

Mean and standard deviation are usually appropriate for roughly symmetric data.

Review Q4 — Normality Test

A normality test gives p-value = 0.004. At the 5% level, what do we conclude?

Since \(0.004 < 0.05\), reject \(H_0\). The data do not appear normally distributed.
